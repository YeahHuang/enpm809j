History

After winning the All Valley Karate tournament in 1982 and 1983, and not losing a single point in the latter, Johnny Lawerence led a series of successful careers before deciding to open up the Cobra Kai dojo to teach today's troubled youth the kind of karate that he learned.

Despite meager beginnings the dojo quickly established itself as one of the top dojos in California, and the United States.

With the demand for spots in the dojo high and COVID-19 restricting in person training sessions has created a new online platform to stream live and on-demand training sessions to members all around the world, something truly disruptive in the karate world.
